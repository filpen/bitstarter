Seatbelt

The virtual pair programming hub.

Find or offer help with technical tasks.

Spread the word!

Do you need help with a programming task and nobody but your cat is around? Would you like to start contributing to open source but the task looks overwhelming? Do you feel like helping or mentoring a developer?

Seatbelt is the meeting point for developers looking for help and offering support using pair programming. Schedule a meeting and start hacking together with your partner!

How it works

  • Post a request for help, or navigate through available requests.
  • When you receive an offer for help with your request, or when you find a request you think you can help with, contact your partner and schedule a meeting.
  • Hack together on the task at hand, learn and have fun!

FAQ

Why would I want to get help from other developers?
While I am sure you can program it all by yourself, working together is the most efficient way to build your technical chops. Learn in a controlled environment with a fast feedback cycle – discuss technical decisions with your partner and find out about possible issues before they come back months or years later to bite you.
Why would I want to help other developers?
You have a chance to help a fellow developer, who will then be extremely grateful to you and will write about you in his personal diary. If that's not enough, do not underestimate the amount of knowledge you get by simply helping. It will motivate you to investigate technical issues that you might have not directly experienced.
